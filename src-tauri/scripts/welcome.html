Welcome to Smart Desktop Manager

This installer will setup Smart Desktop Manager along with its fully integrated, local AI system on your Mac.

What this installer does:

  • Installs the Smart Desktop Manager Core App

  • Downloads necessary LLM models (e.g., Llama 3.2, Gemma)

Because this includes downloading local AI models, the installation may take a few minutes depending on your internet connection. We'll show you exactly what is happening under the hood.
